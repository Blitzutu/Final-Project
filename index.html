    Whats is Belegarth?

    Belegarth is a LARP based loosely on J.R Toilkens World. Players dress up as Knights, Gobylns, Orcs, And Even Elves and enjoy weekends of Camping, Fighting, and Feasting!

    While Most defintely as LARP, as we have Characters that are not human. Alot of the Fighters in the sport are very fighting focused and fighting is only Skill Based. There are no levels, no Classes, and your only as good as you push yourself to be.

    Due to the High Focus on Fighting, Alot of People view this as more of a "Sport" than a "LARP". Its a welcomed for people to go as far to the LARP side as they want, or as far to the Sport side of the game if they want. Or you can be somewhere in the middle. All Varivatiions of Fun are Welcome!

    How do i get Started?

    Finding a Local practice is the easiest way to get started! Most realms will have a Group "Veterans" that will be more than willing to help you start enjoying this Sport!

    Often time these "Realms" will have loaner gear that you can barrow while you test out and find out if belegarth is really for you. There's all types of Different combat, some even if your not too big onto getting hit hard, we still have things like Archery and other types of fighting that is still possibly for you!

    If you know this is something for you, and you want to build your own gear right away you can Visit our Wiki Building Guides here to get some step by step assistance with building your weapons!

    If you can Dream it. You can be it.

    We are a Medivel Fanstay Game. So if you want to stick with the historical side of things or dive completely into the fanstay world, either are welcome! You can test your skills with a varierty Touraments and Competitions, weither it be Artistic or Physical! You can even join one of the many Units in the game, or choose one of the many Paths to Knighthood! Whatever you wish to do, you can find a way to achieve your goals!

    A LARP. Without all the RP.

    So if this is your first time looking into LARP'ing, Heres a couple things you should know. First off LARP Stands for "Live, Action, Role Play". Belegarth is Defintely a LARP. But it moreso focuses on the "Live Action" instead of the "Roleplay". We are a "Full Speed Full Contact" sport where you can also choose to create a fantsy character, but it is not nesicarry. Many players get their own enjoyment out of roleplaying a character while others enjoy just being themselves.

    No Levels. Just your Skills.

    As I touched on previously, Belegarth Shy's away from the regular LARP genre of fighting. One way of doing this is that Belegarth does not have Classes in the sense of "Rangers" or "Barbains" or "Rouges" and you don't have to earn Experience to gain new advangtes. Everything in belegarth is based on your own skill level and how badly you want to improve. You can choose to create a character or just be yourself, either way your skills are based on you. We even have plenty of people willing to share their knowledge you need only ask. Belegarth is what you make of it! And theres not many experiences like it!
